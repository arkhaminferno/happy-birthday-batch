About CelebrateVibes

A music brand dedicated to personalized happy birthday celebrations.

CelebrateVibes was founded with a simple idea: everyone deserves to hear Happy Birthday sung with their own name. We create original birthday songs and matching videos so friends and family can share a personal celebration in seconds.

Our mission

To make birthdays more personal, joyful, and shareable — one name at a time. We publish new names regularly on YouTube so you can search, find, and share instantly.
